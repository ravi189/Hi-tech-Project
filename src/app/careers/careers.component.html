Auto Technician

We are seeking a motivated and driven technician to join our team! You must have your own tools as well as a reliable means of transportation to and from the workplace. At least five years experience working in the professional automotive repair industry is mandatory. Candidates with ASE certification will be preferred.
What to expect: your primary responsibilities as a technician:

  • Be on time, ready to work, and in a clean uniform.
  • Remain friendly and courteous to all clients you are in contact with.
  • Be on time, ready to work, and in a clean uniform.
  • Accurately and honestly troubleshoot client concerns using the technical and administrative resources provided.
  • When present in the front office, act as an auxiliary greeter to clients that have not yet been welcomed.
  • Remain current with any and all technical publications, Service Comm, and Assist terminal operations.
  • Make every effort, within reason, to provide safe and honest vehicle service and repair as promised upon the client’s first visit.
  • Attend to all original client concerns prior to recommending additional repairs or maintenance.
  • Inspect every vehicle for further sales potential, ie: necessary maintenance due by factory recommendations, and /or any repairs that may be required soon.
  • Return clients’ vehicles as clean or cleaner than when it first arrived.
  • Take part in any sponsored training programs available to you.
  • Fill out all paperwork accurately and completely as well as ensure all forms pertaining to the vehicle are kept together and returned to the front office.
  • Address any further duties assigned by the service manager or shop foreman
Minimum Requirements:
  • Valid State driver’s license with good driving record.
  • At least five years experience working in the professional automotive repair industry.
  • Experience performing warranty and non-warranty repairs, inspections, diagnostics, and maintenance services on a variety of vehicle makes and models.
  • First-rate customer service skills as well as general communication skills.
  • Exceptional attention to detail, ability to read and comprehend instructions, critical thinking skills, ability to exercise good judgement in a variety of professional situations.
  • Strong work ethic, with a proven record of achieving and/or over-achieving goals set out before you.
  • Professional appearance, ie, clean uniform and body, well groomed.
  • Positive attitude, high-energy, and amiable personality!
